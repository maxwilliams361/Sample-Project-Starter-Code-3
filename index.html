We need oceans to breath

Oceans produce about 50-80% of the world's oxygen. It also plays a significant role in reducing the amount of carbon dioxide in the atmosphere

Not only are many people throguhout the world dependnet on seafood as a source of food, but many ingredients in foods we eat every day are derived from ingredients we get from the ocean. Products used to fight illnesses like cancer and heart disease can also be found in the ocean
